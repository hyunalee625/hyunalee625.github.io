About me

Goal-driven software engineer with a huge passion for baking, music, and technology and love for animals. As a Business Strategy and Operations manager, I oversaw multiple projects and delegated tasks to the engineering and design teams to build websites and social platforms for clients. I found joy in my work and wanted to learn web development in-depth. Hesitant at first with what I was going to do, but I took the chance and applied to App Academy as my interest grew and overpowered the concerns. As I continued through the program, I realized full-stack development is what I want to pursue. My biggest achievement is to have enrolled in App Academy where I have obtained valuable skills and deepened my knowledge in the technical aspect.

I keep an open mind to learn new things and share positivity and empowerment to others in the present society. I am confident in my ability to go to great lengths to grow and push myself further.
